Welcome to our site!

Welcome to Chez Fur, where you can enjoy delightful coffee and snacks. Take your time to relax as our charming furry hosts entertain you, whether they're frolicking in our outdoor Katio or lounging in the cozy Kitty Kondo. While some of our cats might be a little shy, others are eager to say hello and might even ask for a gentle scratch behind the ears. Just follow their lead and savor the moments spent with our affectionate companions. You may just find a cuddly kitty that wants to make you their new friend!

Reservations are highly recommended.
